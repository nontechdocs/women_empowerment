Safety for Young Girls

Before starting the project I have visited the Sachivalayam in our area to get the permission to conduct the Community service project about Women Empowerment in the Face of Crime. On Day-2 we started conducting survey to know about the awareness people already have on these violence faced by women. On that day we have realized that some people doesn’t even know about those crimes. The next day we met more people to gather their views on how a woman can save herself from these violent situations. On the last day of the week we planned how to spread awareness and to explain some instructions related to Crimes Faced by Women and few techniques to protect themselves. I also included the following concepts in the awareness program

1. Making people learn about the crimes that are committed against women
